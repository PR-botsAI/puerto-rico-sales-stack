## Reading Order Recommendations

**If you have 30 days to get sharp:**
1. *How to Win Friends and Influence People* (week 1)
2. *Influence* by Cialdini (week 2)
3. *Never Split the Difference* (week 3)
4. *$100M Offers* (week 4)

**If you sell big-ticket / B2B:**
*SPIN Selling* → *The Challenger Sale* → *Strategic Selling* → *Mastering the Complex Sale*

**If you sell transactional / retail / one-call close:**
*How to Master the Art of Selling* → *The Psychology of Selling* → *The Little Red Book of Selling* → *$100M Offers*

**If you build a team:**
*The One Minute Manager* → *Ultimate Sales Machine* → *Predictable Revenue* → *$100M Leads*
