## How These Relate to the Books

Strategies are **synthesized**. A single document like `objection-handling.md` pulls from:
- Tom Hopkins' classic objection scripts
- Carnegie's principles of agreement
- Cialdini's commitment/consistency
- Voss's labeling + mirroring
- Hormozi's *risk reversal as objection prevention*

Think of `BOOKS/` as the **library** and `STRATEGIES/` as the **playbook**.
